# Vegetarian Pizza

**Ingredients:**
- Tomato sauce
- Mozzarella (shredded or fresh)
- Sliced bell peppers
- Red onion slices
- Black olives
- Mushrooms
- Olive oil
- Fresh basil or arugula (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F).
2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
3. Add mozzarella, then top with vegetables.
4. Drizzle with olive oil.
5. Bake for 10–12 minutes until vegetables are roasted and crust is golden.
6. Optionally, add fresh basil or arugula before serving.
